Programs · Fractional Chief AI Officer

An executive owns your AI strategy. Engineers build it. Your team actually uses it.

The Fractional CAIO is our core partnership: a hands-on AI department embedded in your company, every month. Strategy, builds, and training, run for you. For companies doing $10M to $500M.

What you get

It's not a workshop. It's not a course. It's your AI department.

01

An executive who owns it

Your fractional Chief AI Officer leads strategy, decides what gets built and in what order, and answers for results. AI stops being everyone's side project and becomes someone's job.

02

A real engineering team

For what Claude can't do out of the box: phone agents, intake pipelines, systems that don't talk. A 15-engineer team led by our CTO, who built the Thursday Night Football backend at Amazon, and our head of engineering out of Microsoft. They ship it, maintain it. You own every line.

03

Your team, fluent in Claude

Claude handles most busywork out of the box. Rollouts still stall at 15% because nobody keeps up with what it can do for their role. We train every person on their actual work until they find their own wins.

04

Company Brain

How the engagement runs

Month one has a deliverable. Then it just runs.

Month 1

The 80/20 Report

We map every workflow in your company and hand you a written report: exactly where AI pays off fastest, ranked, with a build roadmap for the year. It becomes the input for everything that gets built. No guessing, no pilot purgatory.

Monthly

Two tracks in parallel

Our engineers ship the builds the roadmap calls for, anything from agents and automations to data pipelines that pull your operational data into one warehouse. Your team gets trained, role by role, with consultant access by scheduled calls and a shared Slack channel. And you get a weekly strategy call with your CAIO, so AI stays someone's job, not everyone's side project.

Per build

Credits, simply

We write a one-page scope for each build with the objective, hours, and expected outcome. You approve it in writing. We ship it. You can see your credit balance anytime.

The deal

An annual partnership

It flexes by depth, from a few builds a quarter to running your whole AI org, backed by a 60-day money-back guarantee. If you want a one-time project with a finish line, that's the Deployment Program. If you want AI owned, this is it.
