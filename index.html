Questions

The things people actually ask.

Is this consulting, or do you actually build things?
We build. Over 200 shipped projects: custom agents, automations, and internal tools, live in the workflows your people already use. The strategy work exists to point the building in the right direction, not to replace it.
How fast do we see something working?
Your team is embedded in week one, and most clients have working agents inside the first month. We start with the 20% of context and workflows that drive 80% of the value, so the early wins come fast.
What happens to Company Brain if we leave?
It's yours. The structure, the content, and the source mappings live in your environment. We run it while we're engaged, and your admins can edit it any time.
We're not a tech company. Does this work for us?
That's our specialty. Construction, insurance, healthcare, manufacturing, consumer brands. The less digitally native the industry, the bigger the gap between you and the first competitor who closes it.
What does it cost?
A fraction of building the team in-house, which runs $1M+ a year before you've shipped anything. We'll give you a real number on the first call once we understand your size and scope. Every engagement is backed by a 60-day money-back guarantee.
